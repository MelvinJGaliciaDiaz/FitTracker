# 📊 About FitTrack

FitTrack is a **self-hosted personal fitness tracker** built to be simple, private, and free.

Unlike traditional fitness apps that charge subscriptions or sell your data, FitTrack runs entirely as a **web dashboard you control**.

You can open it on **any device**, or add it to your phone's home screen so it behaves like a native app.

The goal is simple:

> Provide the essential tools needed to lose weight and track progress — without clutter, paywalls, or privacy concerns. 

---

# ✨ Features

## 🍽️ Food Logging

Track calories for every meal.

* Breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snack categories
* Running **daily calorie total**
* Compare against **daily calorie goal**

---

## 🔥 Cardio Tracking

Log cardio workouts and calculate calories burned automatically.

Supported machines:

* Treadmill
* Bike
* Elliptical
* Stair Climber
* Rowing Machine
* Jump Rope
* Swimming

Calories burned are estimated based on **body weight and activity**.

---

## 📉 Weight Progress Tracking

Track weight over time with clean visual charts.

Features:

* Log weight entries anytime
* View progress charts
* Switch between **30 / 60 / 90 day views**
* See total weight change since starting

---

## 🔒 Privacy First

Your fitness data belongs to **you only**.

FitTrack guarantees:

* ❌ No ads
* ❌ No data selling
* ❌ No tracking
* ✅ Fully private accounts
